A hand-tracking experiment with Chaucer

How It Works

This page uses your webcam to track your hands. Raise both hands, spread your fingers, and watch Chaucer's words appear at your fingertips.

I.Click Open camera below to grant camera access.
II.Raise both hands with fingers spread — one word appears per fingertip.
III.Shake your hands to change the quote!

Your camera feed never leaves your device. All processing happens in your browser.
